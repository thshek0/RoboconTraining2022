Instruction

1. Merge with other 1-2 groups, you will have to program at least one of the robots in 108A.

2. View the corresponding video below to understand the goal of the task. You may also connect the robot with battery to see what it exactly does.

3. Apply what you have learnt in the last hour (Hint: digitalRead/Write).

4. Think and design the state transit table (similar to the final exapmple).

5. Program it on your Arduino IDE. (Note: Please do not remove the wire, only replace your nano with the existing one. DONOT erase the existing code)

6. Good luck Have fun:)
